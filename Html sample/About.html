Mora Exams

Mora Exam is Srilanka's biggest pilot examination for G.C.E A/L Students. It is organized and conducted by Tamil students of Moratuwa University E-Faculty with helps from many teachers and sponsors. 2024 is the 15th consecutive year of this exam. This exam is totally a voulenteer service without expecting profit. The main reason of this exam is that providing A/L students a model of Final exam to face exam with less pressure as A/L exam is one of the Important exam in a student's life. The number of students taking the exam has been increasing over the years shows the standard of this exam.

Mora exam started with Combined mathematics, Physics and Chemistry and now it is expanded to Biology and IT also. Papers have been set by famous subject teachers of Jaffna. This exam papers wi be conducted in Tami and Engish medium. After exam schemes wil be reeased within 2 days in official Mora exam website. And corrected papers will be distributed in a week. You can also see exam results in webpage and Z-Score also. This year 7000+ students from all 25 disticts are expected to take the exam.
